Data that goes deep... and wide
No more reliance upon limited data sets and archaic approaches. You can now leverage 14 diverse, external data source types. We’ve got everything from consumer conversations to research papers. And we cover over 28K specific data sources, from your favorite grocery platform to expert food blogs.
By food professionals for food professionals
Our team consists of Trendologists, Food Scientists, and Researchers all of whom are nuts [sorry, couldn’t resist] about food! They work closely with our computer nerds [AKA Data Scientists] to ensure our insights are built upon a fundamental understanding of food. For example, our AI, #foodbrain, knows how compatible any two ingredients are and how consumers feel about the pair. Also, #foodbrain can instantly tell you how novel any ingredient pair is, where it’s been applied commercially and socially, and how.
Easy to digest data
Gone are the days of long, boring reports and endless data dumps that drain your brain. Get curated answers, clean dashboards, and insightful scores that mean decision-making is a breeze. Spoonshot’s proprietary scores include Consumer Score for sentiment analysis, Flavor Score for ingredient compatibility, and Novelty Score for product or recipe penetration.
An instant competitive advantage
Spoonshot’s platform gives you a competitive edge by delivering highly personalized insights just for you. Just like Netflix, we’ve developed a recommendation engine that understands what you care about and puts it in front of you when you need it. This also means that you don’t have to worry about your competitors always getting the same data as you. Magic!
